"Stars" is the opening track on the debut EP Exquisite Corpse by the band Warpaint. It is one of the very first songs that the band ever wrote. Exquisite Corpse was recorded in roughly two months. The EP was self-released by the band in August 2008. The EP was subsequently re-released worldwide by the record label Manimal Vinyl on the 6th of October 2009.

The engineer and producer of the EP was Jacob Bercovici while mixing was done by Adam Samuels and additional mixing and mastering were performed by John Frusciante. Frusciante also played mellotron on the song "Billie Holiday" although this detail was not included in the liner notes.

Meet the band

Emily

Guitar and vocals. Emily Kokal is one of the founding members of of Warpaint along with long-time friend Theresa Wayman as well as Jenny-Lee Lindberg. Warpaint was formed on Valentine's Day 2004. The inception of the band took place through a two hour free-form jam.

Emily and Theresa met at the age of eleven when Emily moved from Chico, California to Eugene, Oregon. They used to walk to school together everyday, often singing and harmonising together along the way.

Emily previously played in another band called the Little Two's. She sang lead vocals and played guitar and organ. They released one album in 2001 called World War IV. In 2015, Emily participated in a number of collaborations including with artists Saul Williams and Paul Bergmann.

Stella

Drums. Stella Mozgawa was raised in Sydney, Australia. She began playing the drums at the age of 13. Her parents were also professional musicians and performed as a duo together during her childhood. She initially began learning guitar but later begged her parents to let her play the drums.

Stella has toured, performed and recorded extensively with a wide variety of artists. She has worked with Kurt Vile, the xx, Cate Le Bon, Adam Green, Tom Jones, Kim Gordon, Regina Spektor, among many others. Despite being in such high demand as a musician, she longed for a band to call her own.

Stella moved to LA in 2008 and met Theresa at a party. She was asked to join the band at the end of 2009 and accepted the offer. The first Warpaint recording she performed on was their first studio album The Fool.

Jenny Lee

Bass and vocals. Jenny Lee Lindberg is another founding member of Warpaint. Her sister Shannyn Sossaman was also a member of the original lineup as a drummer and vocalist but she left the band in 2007.

Jenny met Emily and Theresa in Los Angeles at the age of 18. Some of Jenny's influences include Tears For Fears, Siouxsie and the Banshees, PiL, Kraftwerk, Depeche Mode and Aphex Twin.

In December 2015, she released her debut solo album entitled right on! under the name of jennylee. The album was recorded at Happy Ending Studios in LA and released on Rough Trade Records. Jenny and her backing band embarked on a promotional tour of the UK in 2016 following the album's release.

Theresa

Guitar and vocals. Theresa Wayman grew up in Eugene, Oregon. She has been making music from the young age of 9 when she started playing guitar, piano and drums. Her early influences were Talking Heads, Cyndi Lauper and Tina Turner.

Apart from Warpaint, Theresa has also played with Vincent Gallo's band, another band called BOSS, as well as on her own solo project. As a solo artist, she performs under the name TT and she released her first album LoveLaws on the 18th of May 2018. Theresa has also acted in a few movies. She starred in The Rules of Attraction (2002) as well as two short films Rift (2004) and Pete Smalls Is Dead (2010).
