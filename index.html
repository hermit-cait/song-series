The Song Series

This is a small collection of web projects exploring the mobile first strategy and responsive design.

I built them with the intention of practising grid layouts and the art of writing clean code.

These three projects are each inspired by a song. My goal was to create a design for each one that evokes the spirit of the chosen song as much as possible.

In all three I used the lyrics as a key feature. I also included pictures which complement the mood of the song as well as relevant information about the album and artist in question.
